Welcome to the Savoie Research Group

We are a computational chemistry research group focused on developing and applying computational methods to understand and design materials for energy, catalysis, and sustainability applications.

About Our Research

Our research combines quantum chemistry, molecular dynamics, and machine learning to tackle fundamental challenges in chemistry and materials science. We develop new computational tools and apply them to problems ranging from reaction mechanism discovery to property prediction.

Principal Investigator

Professor

Our group develops and applies computational methods to study chemical reactions, materials properties, and molecular systems.
